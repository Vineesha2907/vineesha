VIJAY DEVARAKONDA

Deverakonda Vijay Sai, professionally known as Vijay Deverakonda,is an Indian Actor, film producer, and businessman who works predominantly in Telugu films along with a few Hindi and Tamil films. One of the highest-paid actors of Telugu cinema, he has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2018 and has received several accolades including a Filmfare Award, a Nandi Award, and a SIIMA Award. Deverakonda made his debut with Nuvvila and received praise for his role in the coming-of-age drama Yevade Subramanyam. He rose to prominence as a leading film actor by starring in the blockbusters pelli choopulu and arjun reddy, winning the Filmfare Award for best actor telugu for his performance as a volatile surgeon in the latter. He further established himself with commercially successful films such as Mahanati , Geetha Govindam , Taxiwaala , and Kushi. In 2019, Deverakonda launched his own production house King of the hill entertainment, which notably backed Meeku Maathrame Cheptha (2019) and Pushpaka Vimanam . Beyond his film career, Deverakonda designed his own fashion brand Rowdy Wear, which premiered on Myntra in , and is the co-owner of the volleyball team Hyderabad Black Hawks.
